## Overview

Each SigNoz Cloud workspace has a URL with a subdomain that is auto-generated at creation. You can customize this subdomain to something more recognizable from your workspace settings.

## Prerequisites

- You must have an **Admin** role in the workspace.

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Settings > Workspace** in your SigNoz Cloud instance.
2. Click **Edit workspace link**.
3. Enter your preferred subdomain (minimum 3 characters).
4. Click **Apply Changes**.

## Validate

After clicking **Apply Changes**, a confirmation notification appears indicating your workspace URL is being updated. Once complete:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Workspace** and confirm the subdomain reflects your change.
2. Open the new URL (e.g., `https://your-subdomain.signoz.cloud`) in your browser and verify you can access the workspace.
