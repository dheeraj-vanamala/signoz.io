Ingestion Limits let you place hard caps on telemetry data ingested per ingestion key — independent of what the applications are sending. This is a SigNoz Cloud-only feature.

The primary use case is **non-production environment governance**: prevent a misconfigured dev or staging service from generating an unexpectedly large bill by capping ingestion before it reaches your paid quota.

## How to Set a Limit

Limits are configured per-signal on an ingestion key. The full step-by-step procedure is in the ingestion keys documentation:

**[Add Limits to an Ingestion Key](https://signoz.io/docs/ingestion/signoz-cloud/keys/#add-limits-to-a-key)**

In summary:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Ingestion Settings** in SigNoz Cloud.
2. Click the target ingestion key to open its dropdown.
3. Select the signal to limit (Logs, Traces, or Metrics).
4. Click **+ Limits**, enter a limit value, and click **Save**.

Limits reset daily. Once the limit is hit within the day, SigNoz drops further data for that signal until midnight UTC.

<Admonition type="warning">
  Data dropped due to ingestion limits is permanently lost — it does not buffer or retry. Apply
  limits only on environments where data loss is acceptable (Dev, Staging).
</Admonition>
