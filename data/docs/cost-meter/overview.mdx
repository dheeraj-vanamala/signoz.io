Cost Meter in SigNoz helps you monitor and control your observability spend. Get real-time visibility into data ingestion across logs, traces, and metrics so you can understand cost drivers, spot unexpected spikes, and take action before bills grow.

<Figure

  caption="Cost Meter dashboard"
/>

## Key capabilities

- **[Real-time cost dashboard](https://signoz.io/docs/cost-meter/cost-meter-dashboard/)**: Track ingestion volumes and costs for logs, traces, and metrics with hourly granularity — broken down by record count, span count, datapoint count, and size.
- **[Meter Explorer](https://signoz.io/docs/cost-meter/meter-explorer/)**: Query your usage data with filters and groupings to identify which services, teams, or attributes drive the most ingestion.
- **[Cost alerts](https://signoz.io/docs/cost-meter/alerts/)**: Set threshold alerts on ingestion volumes or costs and get notified before you exceed your budget.

## Get Started

- [Cost Meter Dashboard](https://signoz.io/docs/cost-meter/cost-meter-dashboard/) — Track ingestion volumes across logs, traces, and metrics, compare costs by signal, and spot usage spikes.
- [Meter Explorer](https://signoz.io/docs/cost-meter/meter-explorer/) — Query raw usage data with filters, groupings, and custom time windows.
- [Meter Alerts](https://signoz.io/docs/cost-meter/alerts/) — Set up alerts for spend thresholds, per-signal limits, and ingestion quotas.
- [Meter Explorer Query Guide](https://signoz.io/docs/cost-meter/meter-explorer-query-guide/) — Example queries and saved view workflows.
- [Alert Configuration Guide](https://signoz.io/docs/cost-meter/alert-configuration-guide/) — Common cost alert scenarios.
